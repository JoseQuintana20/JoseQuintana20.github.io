About Me

I am an electronic engineer currently pursuing a master's degree in electrical engineering. My passion lies in designing advanced control systems, with a particular interest in unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) and artificial intelligence applications. My research focuses on adaptive control, trajectory prediction methods, and integrating computer vision for autonomous navigation in complex environments.

Projects

Adaptive Control for Autonomous Drones

Development of an adaptive LQR controller with real-time prediction for obstacle avoidance in UAVs. This project uses Kalman filters and dynamic models to ensure safe and efficient navigation in dynamic environments.

Implementation of Monitoring Systems

Designed and integrated systems for real-time monitoring and control of industrial processes, focusing on precision and scalability.
