About Me

I am an electronic engineer and a master’s student specializing in advanced control systems and autonomous navigation technologies. My passion is integrating AI and IoT to create innovative solutions for UAVs and other complex systems. Through academic and professional experiences, I have developed strong skills in research, prototype development, and multidisciplinary project management.

Academic Background

  • Electronics Engineering: Built a strong foundation in electronics, programming, and embedded systems during my undergraduate studies.
  • Master’s in Electrical Engineering: Focusing on autonomous vehicles and IoT, with research in system identification, state estimation, and control systems for drones navigating complex environments.

Experience

  • IoT and AI Development: Created natural language processing chatbots and predictive models for real estate management.
  • Research in UAVs: Developed trajectory tracking algorithms, integrated 3D mapping systems, and addressed adaptive control challenges for autonomous drones.
  • Data Science Applications: Conducted exploratory data analysis and applied machine learning techniques for academic projects.
  • Research Groups: Active member of the Automatic Control Research Group, focusing on adaptive and optimal control, state estimation, and system identification.
